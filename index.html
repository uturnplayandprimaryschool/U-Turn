About Us

Founded in 2023, U-Turn School combines traditional and modern teaching techniques to nurture well-rounded individuals. We focus on practical knowledge, mental and physical well-being, and essential life skills to prepare students for a bright future.

Our Motto

We are committed to skill-oriented and practical education. Our goal is to empower students to apply their knowledge in real-life situations, fostering innovation, entrepreneurship, and personal growth. We strive to create happy, self-reliant individuals who live balanced and healthy lives.

Subjects We Offer

  • Mathematics: Syllabus, Aptitude, Reasoning, Problem Solving
  • English: Reading, Writing, Public Speaking
  • Computers: MS Office, Typing, Coding
  • Science: Physics, Chemistry
  • Social Sciences: History, Polity, Economy, and Finance
  • Life Skills: Psychology, Diet, Nutrition, Health, and Fitness
  • Fitness and Wellness: Meditation, Yoga, Sports
